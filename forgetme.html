What data we hold

PastePort is designed to minimise the data we hold. Here is a complete list of what is stored on our servers and what happens when you submit a deletion request.

Data On deletion
Email address
Used to identify your account		 Deleted
Encrypted message payloads
Content is E2E-encrypted; we cannot read it		 Deleted
Device registration tokens
Used to route messages to your devices		 Deleted
Subscription / billing records
Only if you have a paid plan		 Anonymised

Billing records are anonymised (not deleted) where required for legal and tax compliance. No personal identifiers remain in anonymised records.

Note on encrypted content

Your encryption key never leaves your devices. Even before deletion, the server cannot read any content you have shared. Messages are also automatically purged after 7 days (Free) or 30 days (Pro).

Processing time

We will confirm receipt by email and complete your request within 30 days. For urgent requests, email privacy@pasteport.app directly.
