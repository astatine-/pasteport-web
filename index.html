Features

Everything in one share

Works the way your brain already does — grab something, send it, move on.

🔗

Any content type

Links, text snippets, images, and files. If you can share it from an app on Android, you can send it to your other devices.

🔐

End-to-end encrypted

Your content is encrypted on your device before it goes anywhere. Our servers only ever see ciphertext — we literally cannot read what you're sharing.

⚡

Arrives in seconds

Real-time delivery over a live connection. Copy on your phone and it's waiting on your laptop before you've switched windows.

🛠️

For every setup

Android app for your phone. Chrome extension for your browser. A full CLI tool for your terminal. All three talk to each other.

📂

History and export

Everything you've sent or received is saved locally and in your account. Browse it later, export it, or clear it — it's your data.

📴

Works offline too

Send to a device that's off right now. PastePort queues the message and delivers it the moment that device comes back online.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

Three steps and your devices are talking to each other.

  1. 1

    Install on your devices

    Grab the Android app from the Play Store and the Chrome extension from the Web Store. Or use the CLI tool if you live in the terminal.

  2. 2

    Sign in and pair

    Create a free account, then pair your devices. Scan a QR code from one device to another, or use a sign-in link — takes about thirty seconds.

  3. 3

    Share anything

    Use the Android share sheet, right-click in Chrome, or pipe something into the CLI. Content appears on your other devices immediately.

Privacy & Security

We can't read your data.
By design.

PastePort uses AES-256-GCM end-to-end encryption. Your encryption key lives on your devices and nowhere else.

✓

Client-side encryption. Content is encrypted before it leaves your device. The server receives — and stores — only ciphertext.

✓

Keys never leave your devices. Your account encryption key is transferred between your own devices via ECDH key exchange. Stori Innovations never holds a copy.

✓

Automatic expiry. Messages are automatically purged after 7 days (Free) or 30 days (Pro). Or delete everything instantly, anytime you want.

✓

No ads, no tracking, no selling data. PastePort is a paid utility. Your content is not the product.
